iOS Shortcut

Please only use this shortcut under the supervision of a Cyber Essentials Plus auditor. It has been designed and tested to support the assessment of your device.

This shortcut automates a small number of actions on your device. When opened, you will be shown a disclaimer with further information.

What does this shortcut do?

The shortcut navigates to specific device settings and takes screenshots as evidence that required security controls are in place. It is designed to minimise the capture of personal data by only recording screenshots of necessary pages.

Once complete, the shortcut opens two web pages containing harmless test files. These files do not affect your device and are used only to confirm that malware protection is working as expected.

If you have any questions before running the shortcut, please speak to your assessor, who will be happy to help.
